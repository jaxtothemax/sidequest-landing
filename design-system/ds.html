01 · FOUNDATION

Design System

A scalable, token-driven foundation for the SideQuest iOS app. Every component is spec'd with anatomy and states so design and engineering speak the same language.

01 · Color

Color

Two hero accents — red #E62C5A and blue #3B82F6 — inherited from the logo. Neutrals are cool-leaning to sit cleanly with both. Every semantic token has a light and dark value.

Ramps

Ramps are always 50-700, evenly spaced. Use 500 for default, 600 for press, 700 for emphasis; 100-200 for tints; 50 for washes.

03 · Spacing

Spacing

A strict 4-point grid. Use tokens, never raw values. Small numbers for intra-component; larger numbers for layout.

04 · Radius

07 · Icons

Iconography

We standardize on Lucide — clean, consistent 24×24 stroke icons with 2px weight. They map mentally to SF Symbols, giving iOS devs a familiar system. Import specific glyphs; don't bundle the whole set.
