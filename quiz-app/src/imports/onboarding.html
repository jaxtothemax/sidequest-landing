Notes for engineering & next iterations

All steps skippable except Conference

Skip uses sensible defaults. Track skip rate per step — anything > 30% skip is a candidate to delete or merge.

Progress feels short

10 dots looks long; we use a thin segmented bar instead. Visually compresses 10 steps into ~70% of the bar at start.

Adaptive copy

Goals + Topics steps re-phrase based on the role chosen ("As a Founder…" / "As a Journalist…"). Tiny detail; big difference in feel.

Resume-on-relaunch

Drop-off is real on multi-step flows. Persist state at every Continue tap; resume mid-flow on app relaunch.

Re-onboarding for new conferences

When a returning user adds a new conference, only re-ask: Days, Goals (pre-filled, editable), Must-haves. Skip role/topics/style.

Chosen variants

Welcome A · Role A · Topics B (rich cards) · Style A (sliders) feel strongest as a set. Final call yours.
